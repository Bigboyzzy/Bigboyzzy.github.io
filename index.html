I am a first year PhD student in Computer College, Harbin Institute of Technology. I become a member of Internet of Things and Ubiquitous Intelligence Research Center, 2020. I received my B.S. degree in School of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Shandong University of Science and Technology, 2018.

I am interested in internet of things, deep learning, edge computing, especially combining artificial intelligence with the Internet of Things to solve some exciting and challenging work.
