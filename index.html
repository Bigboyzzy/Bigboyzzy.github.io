I am a first year PhD student in Computer College, Harbin Institute of Technology. I become a member of Internet of Things and Ubiquitous Intelligence Research Center, 2020. My supervisor is Professor Jie Liu. Before that, I received my Engineering Master degree in College of Electronic Information and Optical Engineering of Nankai University, supervised by Professor Guiling Sun. I received my B.S. degree in School of Electronics and Communication Engineering, Shandong University of Science and Technology, 2018.

I am interested in internet of things, deep learning, edge computing, especially in multimodal learning and reasoning for vision, language, knowledge, cognition and action.
