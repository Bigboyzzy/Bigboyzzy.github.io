Ziyang Zhang

I am currently a Postdoctoral Associate in the NESLab at Politecnico di Milano. working with Prof. Luca Mottola. I obtained my Ph.D. degree from Harbin Institute of Technology in 2024, advised by Prof. Jie Liu. I also work closely with Prof. Yang Zhao at Harbin Institute of Technology (Shenzhen).

My main research interest is edge computing, embedded intelligence, with a focus on high-performance and energy-efficent edge DNN inference.

  • DNN Compilation Optimization (POS).
  • Task Scheduling and Resource Allocation (Octopus), (BCEdge), (ECSRL).
  • Collaborative AI (DVFO).
